REWILDING BANYAN

Starting in November 2024, we will donate USD $1 for every direct booking at Banyan Tree Hotels to support Rewilding Banyan—an initiative restoring natural habitats and enhancing biodiversity through pocket forests
These forests are created by planting diverse native species in small urban spaces, helping to regenerate ecosystems, capture carbon, and support local communities. With every stay, guests contribute directly to climate resilience and the protection of nature for future generations.
